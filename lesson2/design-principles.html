Proximity

The webdesigner introduced the principle of proximity in his design by grouping item of like nature together. for instance he grouped text at one side and picture also at different side

Repetition

This web was designed with a repetition of same color and text on the page, the color of their logo was repeated in many of the designs, and moreover the use of white writings were also repeated.

Contrast

This site made use of black text over white backgroud to bring more contrast to the web page
